New York City

Welcome to this website about New York. Look around for tips on what to do in the city, best ways to get around, and information on the city's vibrant boroughs.

The Subway

The Subway is one the most advanced public transit systems in the world. It's train take passengers throughout the greater New York City area at all times, running underneath and across the city's various neighborhoods.

The Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty was originally a gift from France to the United States. It is a symbolic welcome to the country for the millions of people who traveled from their home countries to start a new life or meet their loved ones in America.
